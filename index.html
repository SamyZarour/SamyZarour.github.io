How I keep myself busy

MISN Student LocatorFind friends on Exchange

A basic MVC app with user profiles and user authentification.
This allows McGill students that are doing a semester abroad to find other students from McGill that are currently in the same city.

BluePrintPlan out your degree

It can be tricky to plan your degree, especially when you're trying to include all the advanced classes you wish to take years down the line. Due to missmanageing course requirements or the exclusivity to specific semesters for specific courses. Since advanced classes are hard enough, we with a team of three Software Engineer we built a graphical view
This grid is an attempt to make something nice that works on touch devices. Ignoring hover states when they're not available etc.

ImmersedLet your phone teach you

Immersed provides any smartphone owner with a personalized language learning coach with zero effort from the user, all you need to do is use your phone as any other day. Thanks to an AI agent, this app dynamically decides what you need to learn next to speak another language in the same way as you speak your native tongue. It learns your way of speech by learning from your daily inputs on your phone.
I am the technical member of the team and built the entirety of the app. We use NodeJS, to build the REST API, paired with Django to have access to scikit-learn and nltk. The front end is only Android so far but we hope to have an IOS equivalent soon.

QuoteMeMotivation at every tab

Simple chrome extension that shows you an inspiring and motivational quote at every new tab.
I built this to get my feet wet with Angular and Chrome Extensions. A bit of NodeJs is also involved.

Sorbere Your browser is your teacher

A chrome extension that stops you from accessing unproductive websites unless you answer one or more questions from a quiz of your choosing. This way your procrastination is fuelling your endless pursue for knowledge. Once you answer successfully you have a 15 minutes pass before having to answer another question.
This was built with NodeJS , Angular and Chrome. The motivation for building this app was my desire to expand my kanji vocabulary.

oSusumeAnime Recommendation Engine

Very self-explanatory. But, by entering your myanimelist.com username, or by entering animes manually, you can get a list of anime that you might liked.
This was built using Angular and Django REST Framework. The AI side is done using scikit-learn.
